🚀 Are you a QA/SDET struggling to land your dream job? Your LinkedIn profile might be the bottleneck! 

Getting "All-Star" status on LinkedIn isn't just a vanity metric—it makes you exponentially more visible to recruiters. Here is my 7-step blueprint to bulletproof your profile for the AI era:

1️⃣ Profile Photo 📸 
Upload a professional headshot. It makes you 11x more likely to be found by hiring managers!

2️⃣ Headline 🎯 
Don't just write "QA Engineer". Highlight your impact! Example:
"SDET Manager at Tekion | AI & LLM Test Architect | Playwright • Selenium • RAG Testing | Helping QA Engineers Thrive in the AI Era"

3️⃣ About / Summary ✍️ 
Tell your story! Use a "Builder" narrative that showcases the problems you've solved, not just a list of tasks. 

4️⃣ Experience 💼 
Add your roles (at least 3!) with detailed descriptions. Highlight metrics and tools used. 

5️⃣ Education 🎓 
Add your university details (e.g., B.Tech). It builds trust and helps alumni find you!

6️⃣ Skills 💻 
List at least 5 skills. For QA/SDETs, prioritize: Playwright, Selenium, Python, Java, AI Testing, CI/CD, and Docker. 

7️⃣ Connections 🤝 
You need 50+ connections! Sync your contacts or connect with former colleagues from your previous tech companies.
