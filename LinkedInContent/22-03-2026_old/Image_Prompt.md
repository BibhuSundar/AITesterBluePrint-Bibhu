# Image Prompts for LinkedIn & Medium Posts

These prompts are optimized for AI image generators like Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Leonardo AI to accompany your social media and blog content.

## Option 1: Modern Tech & Growth Vibe 
**(Ideal for LinkedIn to show professionalism and structure)**
**Prompt:** A vibrant, high-quality flat-lay illustration of a modern workspace. A sleek laptop showing a glowing blue checkmark on the screen next to a generic LinkedIn profile layout. Surrounding the laptop are elegant, conceptual floating 3D icons: a camera (Profile Photo), a megaphone (Headline), a briefcase (Experience), a graduation cap (Education), gears (Skills), and a network node (Connections). The lighting is cinematic with soft neon blue and orange accents. Style is modern corporate minimalism, 8k resolution, photorealistic. --ar 16:9

## Option 2: Human-Centric / SDET Focus 
**(Ideal as a Medium header image to build connection)**
**Prompt:** A diverse, confident software testing engineer sitting at a bright, modern cafe desk. They are looking directly at the camera with a welcoming smile. In front of them is an open laptop displaying colorful syntax highlighting of automated testing code. Hovering elegantly in the foreground is a glowing gold "All-Star" badge. Bright natural lighting, cinematic shallow depth of field, optimistic and professional atmosphere. --ar 16:9

## Option 3: Abstract/Conceptual Growth 
**(Great alternative if you want a purely visual eye-catcher)**
**Prompt:** A glowing, futuristic 7-step staircase leading upward toward a glowing gold 3D star emblem at the top. Soft neon lines of code cascade down the steps. Each step features a subtle minimalist tech icon. Dark blue background with vibrant tech-inspired cyan and magenta rim lighting. High tech, cyber, professional growth concept, 4k. --ar 16:9
